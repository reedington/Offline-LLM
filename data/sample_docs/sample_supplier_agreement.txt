Supplier Agreement

BrightMart Retail Ltd agrees to buy packaged grains from Northline Foods. Northline Foods will deliver orders within five business days after receiving a purchase order.

Payment terms are net 30 days from the invoice date. Late payments may attract a 2 percent monthly service charge.

Northline Foods must replace damaged goods reported within 48 hours of delivery. BrightMart must provide photos and the delivery note number when reporting damaged goods.
